HERE'S MY STORY

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer living in Buford, GA. I had studied at the University of Georgia for about 3 years majoring in Biology from 2015-2018. However, I realized that I did not have the passion nor drive to pursue this degree to the end. I looked for other opportunities and found myself drawn to the world of coding.

In the beginning of 2019, I had the most pleasurable experience of learning to become a full stack web developer at DigitalCrafts' four month, full-time immersive coding bootcamp from February to May. Not only did I learn how to code in order to build websites and applications, but I was able to learn how to work and cooperate within a 3 to 4 person team. From this experience, I had the honor and opportunity to meet talented individuals whom I was able to pick up skills and bounce off ideas while working on projects. I am now seeking for opportunuties to put the skills and knowledge to use in software development.

VCR Trivia
